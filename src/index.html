The venue.

Welcome to Pondvalley Manor!

Our venue is a charming wedding barn located in St. Thomas, south of London and just north of harbourfront town Port Stanley.

The ceremony will be outdoors by the pond, so choose your footwear wisely. Please arrive by 3:45pm to take your seats.

Feel free to bring a casual change of clothes if you'd like to roast some marshmallows by the campfire - and don't forget your dance moves for the reception inside the barn!

Accommodation.

The Comfort Inn St. Thomas is conveniently located five minutes away from Pondvalley Manor by car.

Our room block at the Comfort Inn is now closed - but if you are looking for a room, there are very likely rooms still available at the normal rate!

Rooms booked in our block are ground-level with two queen-sized beds, and feature complimentary breakfast, parking, and Wi-Fi.

Shuttle Service

Transportation will be available to and from the venue. A shuttle service will operate:

  • 3:00pm - 4:00pm to bring guests from the Comfort Inn St. Thomas to Pondvalley Manor (every 10 minutes, approximately)
  • 4:00pm - midnight to shuttle guests between Pondvalley Manor and the Comfort Inn (on demand, trips both ways)
  • midnight - 2:00am for return trips to the Comfort Inn (every 10 minutes, approximately).
